My Reviews

Minimalist Sunscreen

I've been using the Minimalist Light Fluid SPF 50 PA++++ Sunscreen, and it's truly impressive. The lightweight, fluid texture absorbs quickly without leaving any white cast, making it ideal for daily use. It's water and sweat-resistant, providing reliable protection even during outdoor activities.

Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin, it offers excellent value at this price point. Overall, a top-notch sunscreen that delivers on its promises. The price is also decent, so overall I liked this product.

Moisturizer

Dr. Sheth's Ceramide & Vitamin C Oil-Free Moisturizer: I'm loving this moisturizer for my oily skin! It's so lightweight and doesn't leave me feeling greasy, plus it has Vitamin C and Ceramides! My skin feels brighter and more hydrated.

However, due to my skin type being acne-prone and dry, it causes some irritation. I prefer it primarily for oily skin types.

Earbuds

Zebronics MIST Wireless Earbuds: These earbuds are great value for the price. The sound quality is clear and they're super comfortable to wear for hours. I'm really impressed with the battery life.

However, they do create echo sometimes and the Bluetooth range is not that long. For this price, this product is decent. If you are looking for average price with decent quality features, then these are a good choice.

Perfume

This perfume is suitable for multiple occasions like colleges and meetings. I personally loved this perfume. If you are looking for a premium perfume scent for a decent price, go for it!

Protein Powder

It also doesn't contain any harmful chemicals like others which can cause problems in your digestion. I can see some difference in my body after using it regularly. If you are looking for good protein powder which doesn't contain chemicals like gluten and others, then this product is suitable for you.
